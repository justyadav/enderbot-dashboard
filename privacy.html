🔒 Privacy Policy

How we handle and protect your data

🔐 Privacy First: We only collect data essential for the Bot's functionality. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

1. Information We Collect

EnderBot collects and processes the following data to provide its services:

  • Server Data: Server ID, name, channel IDs, role IDs
  • User Data: User ID, username, discriminator, avatar URL
  • Message Data: Temporarily cached for edit/delete logging
  • Configuration Data: Server settings, moderation preferences

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Service Delivery: To provide core features
  • Moderation: To track warnings and actions
  • Logging: To maintain audit logs
  • Configuration: To store server settings

3. Data Storage & Security

  • Data is stored securely in MongoDB or in-memory (fallback)
  • We implement industry-standard security measures
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties

4. Data Retention

  • Server configurations are stored until the Bot is removed
  • Moderation logs are stored for accountability
  • Message caches are automatically cleared (max 1000 messages)

5. Your Rights

  • Access: View your server's configuration
  • Modification: Modify your settings
  • Deletion: Clear warnings and reset configurations
  • Opt-out: Remove the Bot from your server

6. Data Removal

If you want your data removed from EnderBot:

  • Server Admins: Remove the Bot from your server
  • Individual Users: Contact a server admin to clear your warnings
  • All Users: Contact us via the support server
📧 Contact: For privacy concerns, reach out through our Support Server.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this policy periodically.
